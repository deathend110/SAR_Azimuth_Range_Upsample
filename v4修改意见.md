
## 排版建议
1. 参考文献多弄点，20-30，至少23篇-这里可以从`papers\Nie 等 - 2025 - One-Bit Synthetic Aperture Radar Imaging Based on Fixed-Threshold With Slow-Time Fluctuations.pdf`和`papers\Zhao 等 - 2019 - One-Bit SAR Imaging Based on Single-Frequency Thresholds.pdf`的参考文献，以及`MD\reference.md`中进行选取。

2. 摘要缩短，`Experiments on seven SAR datasets with 70 paired samples show that the best bidirectional allocations consistently outperform the best unidirectional allocations across all tested budgets, improving PSNR by 0.2856–0.5072 dB and SSIM by 0.0169–0.0221. The advantage remains under zero-threshold, non-random constant-threshold, and random-threshold quantization. Range-compressed spectral analysis further shows lower off-support and directional leakage, supporting bidirectional allocation as an effective strategy for one-bit SAR imaging.` 这段话就可以大幅度精炼，不用描述具体实验过程和实验结果，数字
